# 09 — Monetization

Layered monetization on a free, viral core. No gambling revenue. Pricing in USD;
localized by region. See `02-BUSINESS-MODEL.md` for unit economics.

---

## Plans

### Free Plan — $0
- Unlimited predictions & reputation building
- Follow users/teams/leagues, social feed
- Match Hub: live scores, community split, discussions
- Basic profile, badges, global leaderboard
- Ad-supported
- **Goal:** maximize reach and the prediction habit (top of funnel).

### Pro Plan — $9/mo (or $90/yr)
- Everything in Free, **ad-light**
- **AI predictions + confidence + previews**
- Premium statistics & historical analytics
- Advanced leaderboards (country/league/club/friends)
- Profile flair, priority support
- **Goal:** convert engaged fans (target 3–5% of MAU).

### Elite Plan — $29/mo (or $290/yr)
- Everything in Pro
- Full historical data exports & deep analytics
- **API-lite** (personal/non-commercial quota)
- Early access to new features, max AI quota
- **Goal:** power users, semi-pro analysts (10–15% of Pro).

---

## Creator subscriptions
- Fans subscribe to creator channels at creator-set prices (`07`).
- **Platform take 20%** (lower at higher creator tiers).
- Yearly options; free preview tiers to drive conversion.

## Advertising
- Native, sports-relevant units in feed and Match Hubs.
- Brand-safe, frequency-capped; removed/reduced for Pro/Elite.

## Sponsored predictions
- Brands sponsor a match's prediction module ("Predict & win — presented by X") — engagement, not wagering.
- Clearly labeled; reputation mechanics unchanged.

## Sponsored communities
- Brands/clubs sponsor or operate official communities (analytics + presence).

## Enterprise API
- **Prediction API · Fan Sentiment API · Community Analytics API** for media, books, fantasy operators, clubs.
- Usage + seat tiers; annual contracts $25k–$250k ACV (`02`, `04`).

## Data licensing
- Aggregated, anonymized prediction & sentiment datasets and indices.
- Privacy-first: no personal data sold; user-controllable.

## White-label products
- Branded fan-engagement / prediction experiences for clubs & media on our infrastructure (Phase 5).

## 5-year financial shape
- **Y1:** invest for growth; revenue immaterial; optimize NSM (WPU) & retention.
- **Y2:** subscriptions + early creator take; approach efficient growth.
- **Y3:** creator economy + enterprise inflect; clear path to operating leverage.
- **Y4–Y5:** high-margin enterprise/data scale; profitability with continued reinvestment.

## Pricing principles
1. Free must be genuinely great — it's the growth engine.
2. Gate **cost** (AI) and **power** (analytics/API), never the core social loop.
3. Annual > monthly (cash + retention).
4. Creator success first — our 20% only grows if they grow.
5. Never monetize via gambling or by selling personal data.
